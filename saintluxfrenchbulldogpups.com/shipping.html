SHIPPING

Serving USA and International pet lovers. If you do not live within driving distance from us don’t let that stop you from getting your French Bulldog from us. *We have an Import/Export License from the United States Fish and Wildlife Service which allows us to ship to most locations. Ask us about details.

Our puppies are shipped Nationwide and Internationally. Prices vary depending on location and are additional to the cost of your dog/puppy. A carrier fee is Included as well. Other fees may be incurred depending on shipping arrangements made. All costs will be determined at the time of purchase negotiation

Upon arrival, plan to open a can of food as they will be very hungry. If you have a long drive home bring a litter box in the car and put them in it. They will arrive in a hard-sided crate with hard kibble dog food. Be sure to check their paperwork for their health record. Before shipping the puppy will see the vet for a health certificate. This will expire in 30 days so they will need to be shipped in this time frame. As soon as we have their schedule and reservation we will give you this information.

With our experience in shipping our dogs, we know for a fact that all puppies are always well-taken care upon transit to their new owners. We have only had a couple of incidences where some of our French Bulldog Puppies had delayed flights or had some minor problems due to a long flight. Measures have been taken to ensure our puppies are transported in the best and most favorable conditions so they get to their new owners in perfect health state.

We do not use Delta Cargo for transporting our puppies because we don't like to transport our them as cargo. We shall be shipping directly from our home where our puppies are bred and raised to any major airport closest to your address, where you will be picking up your French Bulldog. The puppy could also be delivered to your doorstep from the airport. We have Pet delivery expert companies we have been working with

These delivery agencies are a professional pet transporting service by air only (they do not use cargo). They have Pet Nannies that fly via commercial airlines alongside your puppies to the closest major airport where you can pick up your dog. The Pet Nanny flies with the puppy in-cabin of the airplane throughout their journey hand-in-hand to their final destination, and deliver to the receiver.

PAYMENT TERMS

We reserve the right to provide whatever payment method we feel comfortable with based on our sole discretion.

When the full payment including transportation fee is made and confirmed, we will proceed with processing the puppy's new papers, paperwork is usually done within 12 hrs max after which the puppy is registered for transportation. Once the transportation arrangements are done and the puppy's flight booked, all parties will receive a confirmation email that the puppy’s transport is scheduled and booked.

24 – 48 hours prior to the start of the transport, the puppy’s itinerary will be emailed to all parties (shipper and receiver). The itinerary will include all the information needed for both shipper and receiver to fulfill their roles in the transport process including transport date, time of arrival at airport, meeting location for pick up and Pet Nanny’s contact information. (Itineraries may not be given before this time period)
